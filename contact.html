Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the questions we get asked the most. If you cannot find your answer here, please contact us directly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can try GoldenHub CRM free for 14 days with no credit card required. You get access to all Professional plan features during your trial period.

Can I import my existing contacts?

Absolutely. You can import contacts from a CSV or Excel file, or sync them directly from Gmail or Outlook. Our team will help you during your onboarding session.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are fully set up and using GoldenHub within one week. We provide a free onboarding session to help you get started as quickly as possible.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel at any time. There are no long-term contracts. Your subscription runs month to month and you can cancel before your next billing date.

Is my data safe?

Your data is stored securely in our servers. We use encryption to protect all data in transit and at rest. We will never sell your data to any third parties.

Do you offer training?

Yes. All plans include access to our video tutorial library. The Professional and Enterprise plans also include live training sessions with our team.
