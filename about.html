About GoldenHub CRM

Our story, our mission, and the people behind the platform.

Our Story

GoldenHub CRM was started in 2021 by a small group of people who had worked in sales and marketing for many years. We noticed that most CRM tools were either too complicated or too expensive for small and medium-sized businesses to use properly.

We wanted to build something different — a CRM platform that is easy to learn, affordable to use, and actually helps your team sell more. We spent two years talking to hundreds of sales managers and marketing teams to understand exactly what they needed.

Today, GoldenHub CRM is used by more than 5,000 businesses across South Africa and the rest of Africa. We are proud to be helping local businesses compete with bigger companies by giving them the tools they need to manage their customers better.

What We Stand For
